About

4 teams with 4 members are sitting around a table, answering complicated questions about Science and Maths: a lot of us have been a part of this setup-answering the same type of questions.... But, what if we added a little spice to it?

Why Quiz ?

Imagine; brainstorming as an entrepreneur, struggling to find solutions to that you might face someday! Entrepreneurship Development Cell, present to you Quiz-a-preneur! A quiz event exclusively for school students, where you will face corporate world problems.

Perks

This competition would not only test your knowledge and promptness but would surely instill the feeling of being an entrepreneur-dodging and answering complicated questions for a living, and perhaps be lucky enough to get a chance to win a hoverboard!
